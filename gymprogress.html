GYM PROGRAMS

Beginner Full Body Workout:


Day 1:

Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
Bent-over Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
Plank: 3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds

Day 2:

Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Bench Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 12 reps
Russian Twists: 3 sets of 15 reps (each side)

Intermediate Split Routine:

Day 1 (Upper Body):

Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
Pull-ups: 3 sets to failure
Overhead Press: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 2 (Lower Body):

Back Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets of 10 reps
Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps (each leg)
Seated Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15 reps
Day 3 (Rest or Active Recovery)

Day 4 (Push Day):

Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 10 reps
Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
Tricep Rope Pushdowns: 3 sets of 15 reps
Plank: 3 sets, hold for 60 seconds

Day 5 (Pull Day):

Barbell Bent-over Rows: 4 sets of 10 reps
Face Pulls: 3 sets of 15 reps
Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps (each side)

Day 6 (Leg Day):

Front Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
Leg Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 15 reps (each leg)
Standing Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15 reps

Day 7 (Rest or Active Recovery)

Hardcore Full Body Workout:


Warm-up:
5-10 minutes of dynamic stretching and light cardio (jump rope, jogging, or high knees)

Workout:
Deadlifts:
5 sets x 5 reps

Barbell Squats:
4 sets x 8-10 reps

Weighted Pull-Ups:
4 sets to failure (add weight if needed)

Barbell Bench Press:
4 sets x 8-10 reps

Barbell Bent-Over Rows:
4 sets x 10 reps

Dumbbell Lunges:
3 sets x 12 reps (each leg)

Dips (Weighted if possible):
4 sets to failure

Push Press:
3 sets x 8 reps

Front Squats:
4 sets x 10 reps

Hanging Leg Raises:
3 sets x 15 reps

Farmers Walk:
4 sets x 50 feet (each hand)

Battle Rope Exercises:
3 sets x 30 seconds

Finisher:
13. Box Jumps:
- 3 sets x 15 reps

Cool Down:
Stretching and foam rolling for 10-15 minutes, focusing on the major muscle groups worked during the workout.
